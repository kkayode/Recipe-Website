Moin-Moin

This a Nigerian staple food made with blended beans.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

2 servings

Tags : beef breakfast pancakes food

instructions

step 1

Get your ingredients ready. Peel the beans. Boil and peel the eggs. Debone and flake the fish.

Rinse the banana leaves well and cut the stems. Arrange the stems in the bottom of the pot. Set aside.

step 2

Blend the beans with the peppers, onions and crayfish. Transfer into a sizeable bowl, add some warm water, oil and some seasoning cubes and salt to taste, and Mix well to combine add some water if too thick otherwise youâ€™ll end up with thick and dense moin moin.

step 3

Put the pot containing the stems over medium heat and add a little bit of water. Fold one or two banana leaves into a conical shape, add some bean mixture filling 3/4 way full, add some fish and slices of egg and wrap securely to avoid leakage. And cook for 15 minutes.

ingredients

3 cups brown or white beans, peeled and soaked

1 cup vegetable or canola oil

Banana leaves for wrapping

tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
